Group 10: Katelyn Boss and Danny Tran's Trivia Game

Final Project

Tutorial

This is a trivia game designed by myself and Danny Tran. There are three trivia categories to pick from on the start screen. The player can pick a category and choose from four options on each question. There are five questions in each category. The game will tell you when you've gotten a question correct or have gotten it wrong, then you will receive a score out of five once you've finished all of the questions in your chosen category.
